06 — EU AI Act

Every AI app will need an audit trail. Article 4 is already in force.

We're tracking it. Responding. And building compliance into the protocol from day one.

What's already in force — now, today, no exceptions
February 2025 — Article 4: AI Literacy

Anyone developing or deploying AI systems must ensure adequate AI literacy. Not a recommendation. An obligation. In force since February 2, 2025. Enforcement from August 2026.

February 2025 — Article 5: Prohibited Practices

Social scoring. Manipulative AI. Real-time biometric identification. Prohibited. In force. Fines? Up to €35 million or 7% of global turnover. Whichever is higher.

August 2025 — GPAI Obligations

Providers of general-purpose AI models — transparency, technical documentation, training rules. Applies to all new models from August 2025.

What shifted — and why
High-risk AI systems: from August 2026 to December 2027

The European Commission proposed a shift to December 2027. Standardization bodies failed to create technical standards in time. The delay isn't relief — it's a window for those who start building now.
